The Velluto Story

Born from Italian coffee traditions, Velluto brings you the most luxurious coffee experience. Our name, meaning "velvet" in Italian, reflects the smooth, rich texture of our carefully curated blends.

We work directly with small farms across the world's premier coffee-growing regions to source only the finest beans, which we roast in small batches to perfection.
